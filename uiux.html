Care Discover App

The Care Discover App features a map grid system that displays available daycare centers, each marked with a color-coded pin to indicate availability — a crucial factor for new parents seeking childcare. Drawing from personal experience working with a daycare, I found that spot availability is the most frequently asked question from both parents and providers.
